Black Panther

The black panther is not a distinct species, but rather a melanistic variant (with an excess of melanin) of leopards (Panthera pardus) or jaguars (Panthera onca). These felines are characterized by their completely black fur that hides their typical rosettes, which are only visible up close under the light.
